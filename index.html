Who am I?

My name is Diana Nguyen. I am a high school student who has a great desire to learn and explore the science and technology world. My favorite subjects are Math and Science. I am very determined to pursue my career based off math and science. Technology is extraordinary and there is no end to learning which is one of the very reasons I love about it. There is so much you can do because it's all about building and improving.

My Coding Journey

Growing up, I was never exposed to technology to the touch, but I always observed from afar. Now as an high school student, technology is everywhere. I always had a fascination of how websites were built or how a simple thing like moving a character on the computer screen worked. At the beginning of my journey, I started with Hour of Code. I learned the basics very quickly. Then I progressed to Pencil Code where I had started from a blank slate to creating my very own games or drawings through code. Now, I am operating Git and building my very first github.io as you are reading it right now!
